Objective: Create clean and highly informative interfaces
Process: concept and ideas: In this section of the process I conceptualized what I thought others like myself would like to learn about Apple. I had always wondered why their net worth is so high, so I thought to seek out why. Research: Research is alway a part of my design process and over the years I have gotten somewhat good at doing it. For this specific project, I mainly did stock related research and Apple tech specs reasearch also. Concept and layout of design: At this stage of the process, I sketched a couple of illustrations in thumnails then at a larger scale with side notes of what I thought needed more information.Final touches on Rough Draft Input from Others: I find myself, at times, a bit stuck on how to proceed with perfecting and symplifying my ideas to show information as clear as possible. For this project, I asked an art director to look at it and give me pointers.Final Clean-up: As for many designers, the last step in any design is to perfect everything, by which I mean make sure you are following the grid and branding across your design.
